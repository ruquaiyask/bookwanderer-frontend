Frequently Asked Questions

Are all the books physical copies?
Yes! BookWanderer specializes in physical books. We believe in the magic of holding a real book. We do not currently sell eBooks or PDFs.
How do you verify the condition of pre-loved books?
Every book sold to the library undergoes a strict quality check by our archivists. We ensure all pages are intact, the spine is stable, and any highlighting or notes are kept to an absolute minimum.
Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled from your Account dashboard within 24 hours of placing them, provided they have not yet been dispatched from the vault.
How does the "Sell to Us" feature work?
You can list books you no longer need via the "Sell Books" page. Once approved, you ship them to us, and we credit your account or transfer the funds directly to your linked bank account!

Shipping & Delivery Policy

At BookWanderer, we take immense care in packaging your chosen books so they arrive at your doorstep in the exact condition they left our archives.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Shipping Rates & Estimates

We currently offer Free Standard Shipping on all orders within the country. Standard delivery typically takes 4-7 business days depending on your location.

Expedited shipping (2-3 business days) is available at checkout for an additional flat rate of ₹150.

Packaging Materials

To protect the books and the environment, we use 100% recyclable, weather-resistant materials for all our shipments.
